THE AGENCY AT THE END OF CIVILISATION, STANZA (2014, audio based installation)

This installation comprises of a plinth upon which lie a massive number of speakers and screens showing images from a hundred CCTV cameras, integrated into one piece of art. The installation provides the audience spatial audio experience by the means spoken texts and generative visuals. It engages the audience by merging fictionalized or false narratives with traditional systems of surveillance.

This project consists of real time interpretations of the data of the Internet of Cars project .The data sets are retrieved from the UK car number plate recognition system and are aligned with images from one hundred CCTV cameras.
