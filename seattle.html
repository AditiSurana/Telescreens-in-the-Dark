SEATTLE CRIME CAMS, DRIES DEPOORTER (2015, audio & video based installation)

this installation consists of a number of screens mounted to a wall, each streaming live video feed from publicly accessible, city-owned traffic cameras in Seattle.These surveillance feeds are paired with audio from the police and fire alerts. the system is synched such that the videos show the closest camera to the location described in the audio making this a very unsettling experince. Depoorter tries to address the immense amount of data and surveillance present in the society and what it is capable of.
